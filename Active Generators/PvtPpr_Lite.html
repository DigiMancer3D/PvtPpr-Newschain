Shorten Your Hash Link with TinyURL:

      Shortening hashlinks (the itty.bitty.site link storing PvtPpr data), will allow anyone to have a public Newschain that can be shared easily online. Attaching your PvtPprs with TinyURL does add your servelss-stored papers to a server but it is hiding within the numbers of TinyURL.

      This method can be used in a multitude of ways but can also create a determinable Newschain, a newschain where every entry hash is relatively the same byte size.

      Just like the entire PvtPpr system, authors have to choose to use short-links. Not using this will only increase security + privacy but using shortlinks will help people wanting a public newschain to share their newchains.
      If you use shortlinks in your newschain, back-up your newschain with full hashlinks in case of shortlink censorship.
